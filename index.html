My Web3 Journey

Last year, I started my journey deeper into Web3 by exploring different ecosystems, communities, and on-chain opportunities. Since then, I’ve spent most of my time learning, contributing, and understanding how decentralized communities grow.

Current Focus: Optimum

Recently, I started contributing to Optimum, and the experience has been genuinely exciting. The project feels innovative, the community is highly active, and the environment makes contributing enjoyable. It’s one of the few communities where discussions actually feel alive and engaging.

Past Experience: Plume Mainnet

Before that, I spent nearly a year participating in the Plume mainnet ecosystem. Overall, the experience was very positive — especially the consistency of the staking rewards and the community activity around the network. While I appreciated the ecosystem, the long unstaking lock-up period was one aspect I personally didn’t enjoy much.
